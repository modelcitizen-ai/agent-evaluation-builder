How it works

A streamlined workflow designed for technical teams who need reliable data, not more infrastructure.

1. Upload dataset

Import your model outputs as CSV or XLSX. Support for single outputs or multi-column comparisons.

2. Configure rubric

Define custom criteria: Likert scales, binary checks, or free-text justification fields.

3. Invite reviewers

Onboard internal SMEs or external contractors. Track progress and completion in real-time.

4. Export results

Download structured judgments as CSV for downstream analysis and model fine-tuning.

What you get

Everything you need to manage the human evaluation layer at scale.

Reviewer Tracking

Manage multiple reviewers, assign specific data slices, and monitor individual progress through completion dashboards.

Blind Comparison

Support for side-by-side (A/B) testing with randomized positioning to eliminate reviewer bias in model bake-offs.

Custom Rubrics

Build complex evaluation forms with ratings, multi-select, and required text justifications for every judgment.

Audit Trail

Full accountability with timestamps and reviewer IDs for every single judgment, ensuring data integrity for enterprise use.

RBAC

Role-based access control for Admins, Owners, and Reviewers to keep your evaluation projects secure and organized.

AI Drafting Optional

Optionally use AI to help draft initial rubrics based on your dataset, which you can then refine and lock for reviewers.

What we don't do (by design)

We focus exclusively on the human evaluation layer. We don't compete with your model hosting or monitoring stack.

No Model Execution

We don't run your models, manage endpoints, or select model versions. You bring the outputs; we provide the review environment.

No Automated Regression

We don't claim to automatically detect regressions. We provide the structured human data you need to verify them yourself.

No Black Boxes

We don't use proprietary "evaluator models" to grade your data. The judgments come from your experts, on your terms.
